About CleverCart

Founded by a team of two Indiana University students, CleverCart was created to address the current lack of access to cheap, easy, and healthy eating that so many people face.  ​ CleverCart was born from a conversation with Micah's Mom. She was frustrated with the hassle of trying to find the best prices for groceries, in combination with trying to plan a nutritious diet. Like a typical parent, she would rather spend her time on other things than scanning grocery flyers, searching digital coupons, and planning meals every week. Like many of us, she was also confused by the contradictory information bombarding us with what is nutritious or not. ​
